Postdoctoral Researcher

UCD Department of Physics

Cosmology Group

I study gravitational lensing by galaxy clusters. In particular, I measure the amount that cluster lenses bend background galaxies into arcs as a way to measure the small scale variations in the distribution of both baryonic and dark matter. This bending is called flexion, and is a quantitative measure of gradients in the local lensing convergence and shear fields.
